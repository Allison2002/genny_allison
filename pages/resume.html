AI Content Creator · SEO Specialist · Web Performance Optimizer

I founded Allison Stock Inc. to specialize in SEO-optimized, AI-powered websites built for speed, clarity, and search performance. I merge content strategy, UX, and front-end development with advanced prompt engineering and structured data.

My work bridges the gap between visual branding, semantic HTML, and structured SEO—from initial UX layout to long-form AI content and schema markup that meets modern search engine standards.

AI Content & Prompt Engineering: Built article frameworks, product descriptions, metadata, and structured content with prompt-driven workflows using ChatGPT and Claude. Applied multi-step prompting, role-based inputs, and SEO-focused iteration loops.
