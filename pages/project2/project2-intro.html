project 2 | the website

sports tube

AI Content Creator & SEO Specialist Portfolio Project

Sports Tube is a dynamic platform that blends AI-driven content creation with modern web development to offer insights into the latest technology trends, tools, and innovations. With a sleek, responsive design, the site now scores 99/100 on mobile and 100/100 on desktop in Google PageSpeed Insights, ensuring fast load times and a smooth user experience across all devices.

While recent changes in Google's PageSpeed Insights scoring system, along with my use of Netlify and Cloudinary’s free-tier services, have made it harder to achieve perfect scores, Sports Tube still performs at an exceptional level. It previously held a perfect 100/100 score, and the site remains high-performing, particularly when it comes to SEO and usability.

AI tools like ChatGPT, Midjourney, Relume.io, and Uizard played a huge role in shaping both the design and content of the site. The articles are carefully crafted with AI assistance, and custom visuals, designed with Photoshop and Canva, give the site a professional and visually striking look. The structure of the site was meticulously planned using Figma and Relume.io, with a focus on providing a seamless user experience and making sure the site is accessible to all.

Hosted on Netlify for smooth performance, Sports Tube is a prime example of how AI and web development can work together to create a compelling, engaging platform for tech enthusiasts.
