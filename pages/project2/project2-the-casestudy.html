Sports Tube

AI Content Creator & SEO Specialist Portfolio Project

Case Study: Sports Tube

Role: Front-End Developer, AI Content Creator, SEO & Accessibility Specialist

Website: Sports Tube

GitHub Repository: View the Code

Introduction

Sports Tube is an immersive video platform designed for sports enthusiasts to explore and stream highlights, documentaries, and live events. Driven by my commitment to AI Content Creation, SEO Optimization, and a keen focus on Accessibility, this project demonstrates how a strategic, user-centered approach can transform a site’s performance—from a 35 desktop score to near-perfect metrics—while retaining a compelling user experience.

Throughout this project, I refined my skills as a Front-End Developer, AI Content Specialist, and Accessibility Advocate, ensuring that every aspect of the user journey, from load speed to content clarity, was meticulously optimized.

Objectives

  • Create an intuitive and responsive video platform spotlighting sports-related content.
  • Leverage AI tools to accelerate content creation and optimize SEO.
  • Improve performance from an initial PageSpeed Desktop score of 35 to near-perfect results.
  • Enhance Accessibility to ensure an inclusive user experience.

The Role of AI

AI played a pivotal role in both the content and technical optimization of Sports Tube:

  • ChatGPT: Assisted in drafting SEO-friendly descriptions, headlines, and metadata, ensuring alignment with best practices for increased discoverability.
  • MidJourney and DALL-E: Generated unique, sports-inspired visuals used in hero banners and promotional graphics.
  • Canva: Provided quick enhancements to images and thumbnails to maintain brand consistency and visual appeal.
  • AI-Powered Audits: Leveraged automated SEO and accessibility audit tools to identify areas for improvement in real time.

SEO Optimization

Achieving high visibility and discoverability was key to Sports Tube’s success:

  • Google PageSpeed Insights: Focused on critical render paths, caching strategies, and image compression to boost performance from a 35 desktop score to 100.
  • Keyword Research: Used SEMrush and Google Trends to discover niche keywords for sports content, integrated them naturally into metadata and headings.
  • Structured Data: Implemented schema markup for video content to enhance search engine results display and increase click-through rates.
  • Link Building & On-Page SEO: Created engaging, AI-crafted articles and category pages with relevant internal links, boosting site authority and user dwell time.

Accessibility & User Experience

  • WCAG Compliance: Incorporated sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigation support, and ARIA labels to ensure an inclusive experience for all users.
  • Responsive Design: Adopted a mobile-first approach, guaranteeing consistent functionality on devices of all sizes.
  • Clear UI Controls: Used clear iconography and alt text on videos and images for screen readers and assistive devices.

These measures contributed to an accessibility score consistently in the high 90s, reflecting my commitment to universal design principles.

Development Process

  • Technology Stack: Utilized HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Node.js for dynamic elements. Deployed on Netlify for streamlined CI/CD workflows.
  • Performance Tweaks: Optimized images via Cloudinary transformations, minified CSS/JS, and leveraged caching to dramatically improve page load times.
  • AI & UX Integration: Employed AI-driven analytics to understand user behavior, ensuring interface enhancements based on real data.
  • Quality Assurance: Performed cross-browser testing (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and confirmed no major regressions post-deployment.

Key Results

  • Performance: Elevated from a 35 Desktop Score to 100 Desktop / 99 Mobile on Google PageSpeed Insights.
  • SEO Success: Significantly boosted organic search presence, with targeted keywords ranking on the first page.
  • Accessibility: Achieved an average accessibility score of 97+, showing strong WCAG 2.1 compliance.
  • User Engagement: Increased average session duration by 25% through improved site structure, faster load times, and clearer navigation.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Initial slow performance due to heavy hero video and large images.
    • Solution: Compressed and converted images to next-gen formats (WebP), deferred non-critical JavaScript, and used lazy loading for below-the-fold content.
  • Challenge: Balancing AI-generated content quality with brand voice.
    • Solution: Created an editorial review process to refine AI drafts, maintaining brand consistency and audience relevance.
  • Challenge: Ensuring robust accessibility for video content.
    • Solution: Added captions and transcripts for videos, labeled interactive elements, and verified color contrast across all screens.

Outcome

Sports Tube exemplifies how thoughtful design, AI-enhanced content creation, and rigorous SEO/accessibility strategies can dramatically improve a website’s performance and usability. From a modest starting score to near-perfection on both mobile and desktop, this project underscores my dedication to delivering value across performance, accessibility, and engagement metrics.

This case study showcases my readiness for roles that demand AI Content Creation, SEO excellence, and a deeply informed approach to Accessibility. It also reflects my aspiration to help businesses elevate their online presence through innovative, data-driven solutions.
