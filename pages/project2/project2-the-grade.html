project 2 | the grade

Achieving 100/100 on mobile and 100/100 on desktop in Google PageSpeed Insights highlights my continuous drive for excellence in SEO optimization and performance. This score reflects my ability to adapt to the recent changes in scoring methods and ensures that my websites remain fast and user-friendly across all devices.

Through a combination of AI-driven content creation and advanced web development practices, I improved the site's performance from an initial score of 71 on mobile and 96 on desktop to a 99/100 mobile and 100/100 desktop. This transformation involved:

  • Optimizing images and generating custom AI visuals to balance speed and quality while enhancing the user experience.
  • Refining code for better performance, ensuring that the website functions at peak speed.
  • Structuring content in an SEO-friendly manner, focusing on both technical and on-page SEO strategies to boost visibility and user engagement.
  • Implementing best practices in Core Web Vitals and reducing blocking resources for faster loading times.

These achievements showcase how seamlessly AI content creation, including articles crafted with tools like ChatGPT and images generated by Midjourney, integrate with web development to boost both performance and SEO. This reflects my ability to deliver cutting-edge websites that are fast, functional, and optimized for search engines.

I view this milestone as just the beginning. With every project, I continue to refine my skills in SEO, AI content, and web development, ensuring that each website is not only optimized for performance but also tailored to meet user needs and maximize engagement.
