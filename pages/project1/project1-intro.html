AI Content Creator & SEO Specialist Portfolio Project

Tech Trends Blog merges creative content with cutting-edge web development. Using powerful tools like ChatGPT, Midjourney, and Relume.io, I’ve built a platform that delivers the latest in technology trends while ensuring a fast, user-friendly experience, optimized for SEO and performance across all devices.

The blog’s structure blends modern web development techniques with AI-driven content creation, optimizing for both SEO and performance. This approach provides seamless content delivery and user-centric designs, while incorporating innovative visuals and interactive elements enhanced by AI.

From concept to execution, AI tools like ChatGPT, Midjourney, Relume.io, and Uizard played a pivotal role in shaping both the design and content. Articles were generated with the precision of AI assistance, while custom visuals, inspired by Photoshop and Canva, add a bold, professional touch. The layout was meticulously crafted using Figma and Relume.io, ensuring a focus on user experience and accessibility.

Hosted on Netlify, Tech Trends Blog stands as a testament to the integration of AI content creation and modern web development, blending creativity, functionality, and technology to engage and inform technology enthusiasts.
